This project proposes a Smart RFID and Camera-Based Automated Attendance System designed especially for rural schools. The system combines RFID card scanning with live photo verification and periodic classroom monitoring to ensure accurate and tamper-proof attendance. Students scan their RFID cards at entry, and classroom cameras capture images at regular intervals to confirm continued presence. Attendance data is stored securely for a short duration and parents are notified via SMS alerts in case of absences.
