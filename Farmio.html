My Skills

FARMIO is a smart, inclusive farming platform designed to support farmers with real-time agricultural insights using both feature phones and smartphones. The solution brings together weather updates, soil health information, crop profit prediction, market prices, and early disaster alerts in one simple system. By using satellite data, government open data, and AI-based analysis, FARMIO helps farmers make safer and more profitable farming decisions at a low cost. The platform addresses key gaps in existing agri-tools, such as high cost, complexity, smartphone-only access, and lack of profit or disaster prediction. FARMIO ensures digital inclusion by offering USSD/SMS-based access for feature phone users while providing advanced app features for smartphone users. Overall, the solution aims to reduce crop loss, improve farmer income, save time, and empower rural communities through accessible, data-driven smart farming.
